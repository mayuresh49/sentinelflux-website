Features

Everything a consulting firm needs to run its talent operation

From the first client meeting to the final offboarding checklist — with structured quality gates and health signals at every step.

Global Dashboard

Active clients, open positions, bench vs. deployed headcount, and flagged placements — at a glance. Know your operational health without opening a single spreadsheet.

Clients & Hiring Needs

Track every open role with required skills, experience range, target date, compensation, and status. A 3-phase hiring pipeline keeps every opening moving from sourcing to offer.

Placement Quality Gate

A mandatory 4-point checklist — pre-screen, technical bar, background check, client alignment — that must be cleared before an engineer can be marked Deputed. Clearing it deputes the engineer automatically.

Employee Pulse Reviews

Structured 1-on-1s with 1–5 sliders across stretched / supported / growing, blockers, skill alignment, operations, and delivery. Each review logs to a history and timeline chart on the Talent Profile.

Client Account Health

A color-coded Weekly Engagement Health Signal board — Green, Amber, or Red — per placement. Monthly Retrospective logger for structured account reviews. Never let a client relationship drift without a signal.

Analytics & Action Center

Client-level stats, employee experience distribution, average pulse scores, and skills supply vs. demand. The Action Center surfaces hiring deadlines, incomplete gates, and pending health updates in one view.

Checklists & Offboarding

Onboarding and deputation checklists keep day-one smooth. Engineer and client offboarding checklists ensure nothing is missed when a placement ends — equipment returns, access revocations, and exit reviews included.

RBAC & Audit Log

Four scoped roles — Master Admin, Account Manager (sees only assigned clients), Recruiter, and Viewer — with full audit logs per entity and CSV export. Every action is traceable.

How it works

From client win to engineer offboarding

1

Add the client

Register the client, assign an Account Manager, and create open positions with skill requirements, experience range, and target date.

2

Gate the placement

Clear the 4-point Placement Quality Gate — pre-screen, technical bar, background check, client alignment — before the engineer can be deputed.

3

Run the feedback loops

Log weekly Green/Amber/Red health signals for each placement, conduct monthly retrospectives with clients, and run structured pulse reviews with engineers.

4

Act on signals

The Action Center surfaces every overdue health update, incomplete gate, and upcoming deadline so nothing slips between roles.
