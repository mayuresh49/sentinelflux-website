How this works

The generator is instructed to produce tests in order of importance — critical happy-paths and core business flows first, edge cases and error paths last.

The first of that ordered list are labeled sanity. The remaining are labeled regression.

Result: sanity = your most important scenarios, regression = broader coverage.
