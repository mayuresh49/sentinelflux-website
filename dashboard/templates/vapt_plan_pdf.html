The assessment follows {{ eng.scope.methodology }}. Testing is conducted in a structured, phased approach covering reconnaissance, enumeration, exploitation, and reporting.

PhaseActivityDescription
1ReconnaissanceInformation gathering and attack surface mapping
2Vulnerability AssessmentAutomated and manual scanning for known vulnerabilities
3Penetration TestingActive exploitation of identified vulnerabilities
4AnalysisRisk analysis, CVSS scoring, and remediation guidance
5ReportingDelivery of findings report and executive summary
6Re-validationVerification of remediated findings

7. Rules of Engagement

  • Testing is authorized only against the in-scope systems listed above
  • No denial-of-service or destructive tests will be performed
  • All critical findings will be communicated immediately upon discovery
  • All data accessed during testing will be handled confidentially
  • Testing will be conducted during agreed windows to minimize business impact
