About SentinelFlux

AI-native tools for the
software delivery lifecycle

SentinelFlux is a founder-led studio building AI-powered products — and providing hands-on engineering services — that remove friction from how teams design, build, test, and ship software.

Why We Exist

Too much of shipping software is still manual and error-prone

Writing and maintaining tests. Tracking which API change just broke a downstream team. Watching what AI agents actually do in production. Generating the invoices and letters a business runs on. Each one is a chokepoint — repetitive, easy to get wrong, and rarely anyone's favourite work.

Small and mid-size teams feel it most: they can't staff a specialist for every one of these. SentinelFlux exists to hand that work to software that does it well.

Brittle test suites — UI and API changes break dozens of tests overnight
Silent breakage — contract changes and agent failures found in production
Busywork — hours lost to documents, invoices, and repetitive ops
What We Build

A focused suite of AI-powered tools

Each product tackles a distinct chokepoint in the delivery lifecycle. All are fully hosted and managed — nothing to deploy.

VigilQA
AI-Powered Test Automation

Generate maintainable test suites from your product knowledge base, heal broken locators, and get AI failure analysis across web, API, security, and performance.
SpecVault
API Contract Registry

Publish and version OpenAPI specs, auto-detect breaking changes between releases, and alert subscribed consumer teams before anything breaks in production.
AgentPulse
AI Agent Observability

Alert rules, cost-per-product analytics, and execution traces for AI agent fleets — ingested via a single HTTP call, with no SDK or instrumentation changes.
DocForge
Document & Invoice Generation

Generate employee letters, GST invoices, and contract letters from your data — print-grade PDFs, AI-assisted drafting, and optional electronic signing in one workspace.
TalentDesk
Client & Talent Management

Client and talent management for consulting firms — track clients, hiring needs, and deployments with a placement quality gate, employee pulse reviews, and weekly engagement health signals.
AI does the heavy lifting — humans keep the final say

How We Work

Practical AI, with a human in the loop

We build AI-native tools that understand your product and your data — not generic automation bolted on after the fact. Where the AI proposes a change, a person approves it before it counts.

Every product is delivered as a hosted, managed service, so teams get value on day one without standing up infrastructure. We move quickly and let real usage shape the roadmap.
