AI Drafting

Claude drafts and rewrites letter bodies and contract clauses right inside the Template Studio. It is optional and uses your own API key.

What it does

AI drafting helps with the part people stall on: the free-text body of a letter or the wording of a clause. Ask it to draft from a short prompt, or to rewrite, shorten, or formalise an existing passage — then accept the suggestion into your template or keep editing.

Where it lives

It is built into the Studio, alongside the template you are editing, so drafting happens in context rather than in a separate tool. The model is the latest Claude.

Optional and key-scoped

AI drafting is optional and runs against your own Anthropic API key, so usage is on your account and under your control. It is available on the Pro plan.

Tip: draft the body with AI, then bind the parts that vary per document (names, dates, amounts) to template fields so the structure stays reusable.

Good uses

  • Drafting a warning letter's description from a few bullet points
  • Rewriting an amendment clause to be clearer and more formal
  • Tightening an offer letter's welcome paragraph
