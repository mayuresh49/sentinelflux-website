Contract Letters

A set of contract-management cover letters, consistent and branded, ready to send alongside your agreements.

Included letters

  • NDA cover letter
  • Renewal notice
  • Amendment notice
  • Termination notice
  • Vendor-agreement cover

How they work

Like the HR letters, each is parameterised — counterparty, dates, contract reference, and terms are fields, not hand-edited text. Branding is inherited from the workspace so every notice looks consistent.

Drafting clauses

For clause text — an amendment's specifics, a termination's grounds — use AI drafting to draft and refine wording in the Studio, then accept it into the template.

Signing

Send any contract letter for signature. For agreements that may be contested, route them through a qualified provider; see E-Signing & Audit Trail.
